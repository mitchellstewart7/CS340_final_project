Price Mart

Welcome to Price Mart! The below list includes the ways in which you can use this system to manage the store.

  • The Items tab will allow you to keep track of items in the store.
  • The Customers tab will allow you to manage the store's customers.
  • The Employees tab will allow you to monitor the status of employees at the store.
  • The Orders tab will allow you to look at past orders the store has made.
  • The Titles tab will allow you to manage the titles and their pay rates.
  • The ItemOrders tab will allow you to reference which items wwere in which orders.
